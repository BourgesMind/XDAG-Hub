## Overview

During the development process of the XDag blockchain,
several different wallet software options have emerged.
Currently, the community continues to utilize three main wallets: goXdagWallet, XdagPro, and XdagHub.
So why did the community develop these wallets, and in which scenarios are they most suitable?
This article will cover these topics.

<TOCInline toc={props.toc} exclude="Overview" toHeading={2} />


##  goXdagWallet

- **Suitable for:** Initial XDAG wallet addresses or early addresses without a mnemonic phrase.

- **Url:**  https://github.com/XDagger/goXdagWallet/releases


## XDagPro

- **Suitable for:** A new type of wallet that uses mnemonic phrases, compatible with Android and Apple phones.

- **Url:**  https://github.com/XDagger/XDAG-Pro/releases

- **Important:** Remember to securely store the mnemonic phrases.

## XDag Chrome / Edge Extension Wallet

- **Suitable for:** A new type of wallet that utilizes mnemonic phrases, compatible with Chrome/Edge browsers through extensions, suitable for interactive web app.

- **Url:** Enter the following address in your browser's address bar:https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/xdag-wallet/ilboijfdpoiokmioeiceibgpbnemlkeb

- **Important:** Make sure to back up your mnemonic phrases, as they are required to restore your wallet when reinstalling the browser extension.
